Team Elite Pegasus III
David Yeo & Joe Lee

Final Bidding Price: SGD$250,000

Team Elite Pegasus III David Yeo and Team Elite Pegasus III Joe Lee are from “Team 37”, and “Team 37” has been established for many years and has produced several Team Elite Pegasus Leaders, Golden Deer Award winners and furthermore the team is spread across Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia and other countries, and all these will not be possible if there is no careful management, and patience in their development training. They have always been advocating the motto of "100% Team Work" as they believe that no man can be greater than when in a group, therefore one should always put the interests of the team above oneself.

From the start when building a career in RIWAY, the leaders had set their goals on being the best, and thus when “Team 37” was formed, naturally the goal evolved to aim to be best team in RIWAY. In the process of meeting the challenges to create miracles, the greatest reward gained was being moved by the team mates, because strength in unity is a necessity for a team; and although the team is made up of people from all walks of life, regardless of nationality, race and language barriers, the team mates from “Team 37” relentlessly put in all their efforts, and so it is the underlying reason that touched the leaders the most.

At the charity auction, even though the team only knew about the 3 auction items at the very last moment, they swiftly gathered all the team mates to discuss, after which all of them gave their unanimous support, allowing them to successfully bid for the “Eternal Heart” and present it to Chief Development Officer Dato’ Kenny Wong.

Lastly, they would also like to express their thanks to leaders from “Team 37” for their contribution to the charity auction, and they are Team Elite Pegasus III Yang Zhongfang, Team Elite Pegasus II Hasim, Team Elite Pegasus I: Efendi, Woon Tong Kong, Team Elite Pegasus: Kelvin Kuah, Antono, Donny, Liaw Boon Eng, Team Elite Monoceros: Haven Meng, Team Elite Phoenix: Ben Low, Sam Gwee , Jayden Ng, Team Elite Pavo: Nick Ang, Yanto Hia, Alex Chan, Kwek Ai Ding, Khoo Chye Koon, Jasmond Teo, Yang SuYu, Gary Ng, George Ng, Jason Pang, Andy, Jayson See and Christopel.

Eternal Heart

The “Eternal Heart” pin symbolizes the all-encompassing and limitless expansion, just like Chief Development Officer Dato’ Kenny Wong who is broad-minded. The reasons behind the meaning of presenting “Eternal Heart” to him was because from the start, and even before “Team 37” was established, Chief Development Officer Dato’ Kenny Wong had always relentlessly and patiently given his guidance to the team. Hence, in their hearts, they felt that the “Eternal Heart” pin is the best at representing their sincerity for him as well as being the most qualified person to receive it.

Final Bidding Price: SGD$320,000

The impressions that we have for RIWAY International’s Chief Marketing Officer Claudia Ong, Chief Development Officer Dato’ Kenny Wong and Chief Operations Officer Ivy See have always been one that is full of positive energy and warmth. They always uphold the mission of creating the best environment for everyone, so that RIWAY’s distributors and even the staff can have the best opportunities for growth and development.

Even though they each have different strengths and capabilities, they share a common goal, with their mutual understanding and coordination combining with the other departments and leaders’ abilities, it enabled them to lay a strong and solid groundwork from the start, allowing RIWAY to expand into other countries with a stable and sturdy foundation.

As the Chinese saying goes “In a group of three persons, there will always be someone to learn from”, hence if all three of them are mentors, wouldn’t that be commendable? At this charity auction, the three management leaders unite their efforts in one heart, bidding for the “Eternal Heart”, showing not only their conviction for charity, but also highlighted a leader’s drive and determination.
