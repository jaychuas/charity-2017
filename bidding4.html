RIWAY International
Chief Executive Officer | Dr. Lim Boon Hong

Final Bidding Price: SGD$1,000,000

At the final moments of the charity auction, RIWAY International Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Dr. Lim Boon Hong decided to put up the previous auctioned item “Heart of RIWAY” for bidding, thereafter he won the bid for the “Heart of RIWAY” back again!

Regardless for charity or entrepreneurial aspirations, RIWAY International CEO’s Dr. Lim Boon Hong has always led by example in leading the leaders through difficult times, as he is a pioneer who does not fear any setbacks or obstacles.

At the same time, he is also RIWAY International Core Management’s team leader, always decisive in decisions-making, in problem-solving, and calm in the face of a storm, able to utilize limited resources to achieve the highest efficiency, playing an utmost important role in the management team.

Especially when it comes to charity, Dr. Lim reveals his enthusiasm and loving personality, he is compassionate and sensitive to the needs of the disadvantaged and vulnerable, completely epitomizing the traits of a leader, personality and world view.

Just like the essence of the “Heart of RIWAY”, encompassing RIWAY’s Six Core Values: Financial Goals, Healthy Lifestyle, Social Skills, Family Values, Right Thinking and Spiritual Growth, RIWAY International’s CEO Dr. Lim Boon Hong represents the core of the team, regardless of the size of the team, for the entire organization, illuminating the future, guiding everyone in the right direction.
